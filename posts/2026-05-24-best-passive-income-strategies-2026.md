## What is Passive Income?
Passive income refers to the earnings generated from investments, assets, or businesses that require little to no direct involvement. It's a way to monetize your skills, expertise, or resources without actively working for every dollar. With the increasing trend of online businesses and digital products, the opportunities for passive income have never been more abundant. According to a recent survey, 64% of entrepreneurs believe that passive income is essential for achieving financial independence.

### Types of Passive Income
There are several types of passive income, including:
* Dividend-paying stocks
* Real estate investment trusts (REITs)
* Peer-to-peer lending
* Affiliate marketing
* Online courses and ebooks
* Stock photography
* Royalty-generating assets
