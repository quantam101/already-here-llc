## What is Print on Demand and How Does it Work?
Print on demand is a business model that allows you to design and sell custom products, such as t-shirts, mugs, and phone cases, without holding any inventory. When a customer places an order, the product is printed and shipped directly to them by a third-party supplier. This model eliminates the need for upfront inventory costs and reduces the risk of unsold products. To get started with print on demand, you'll need to choose a niche, design high-quality products, and set up an online store or sales channel.
