## What is Passive Income?
Passive income refers to the earnings generated from investments, businesses, or assets that require little to no direct involvement. It's a way to create wealth without actively trading time for money. The key to successful passive income is to find opportunities that align with your skills, interests, and resources. With the trend velocity of passive income on the rise, it's essential to stay ahead of the curve and capitalize on the most effective strategies.

### Types of Passive Income
There are several types of passive income, including:
* Dividend-paying stocks
* Real estate investment trusts (REITs)
* Peer-to-peer lending
* Affiliate marketing
* Online courses and ebooks
* Renting out a spare room on Airbnb
* Creating and selling an online course or ebook

Each of these options has its unique benefits and drawbacks, and it's crucial to understand the pros and cons before investing your time and money.
