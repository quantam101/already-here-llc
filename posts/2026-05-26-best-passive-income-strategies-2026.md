## What is Passive Income and Why is it Important?
Passive income refers to the earnings generated from investments, assets, or business ventures that require little to no direct involvement or effort. In today's fast-paced world, having a reliable source of passive income can be a game-changer, providing financial stability, peace of mind, and the freedom to pursue your dreams. According to a recent survey, 75% of millennials consider passive income to be a key factor in achieving financial independence.

### Understanding the Benefits of Passive Income
The benefits of passive income are numerous, including:
* Reduced financial stress and anxiety
* Increased financial security and stability
* More free time to pursue hobbies and interests
* Opportunities for long-term wealth creation
* Diversification of income streams
