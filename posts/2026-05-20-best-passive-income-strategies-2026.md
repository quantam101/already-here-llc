## What is Passive Income?
Passive income refers to the earnings generated from investments, businesses, or assets that require minimal effort to maintain. It can be achieved through various means, such as investing in dividend-paying stocks, renting out properties, or creating and selling digital products. The key to successful passive income is to find a strategy that aligns with your skills, interests, and resources.

### Types of Passive Income
There are several types of passive income, including:
* Investment income: generated from investments in stocks, bonds, or real estate
* Rental income: earned from renting out properties or equipment
* Digital income: generated from the sale of digital products, such as ebooks, courses, or software
* Royalty income: earned from the sale of intellectual property, such as music, art, or literature
