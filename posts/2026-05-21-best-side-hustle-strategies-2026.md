## What is a Side Hustle?
A side hustle is a part-time business or venture that allows you to earn extra money outside of your primary job. It can be anything from freelancing, selling products online, to renting out a spare room on Airbnb. With the right mindset and strategies, a side hustle can help you pay off debt, build savings, and achieve your long-term financial goals.

### Benefits of Having a Side Hustle
Having a side hustle can have numerous benefits, including:
* Increased earning potential
* Improved financial security
* Enhanced skills and experience
* Networking opportunities
* Personal fulfillment and satisfaction
