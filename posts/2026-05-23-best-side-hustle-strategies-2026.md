## What is a Side Hustle and Why Do You Need One?
A side hustle is a part-time business or freelance work that you can do outside of your regular job to earn extra money. Having a side hustle can help you pay off debt, build savings, and achieve financial independence. According to a recent survey, 45% of millennials have a side hustle, and the number is expected to grow as more people seek financial freedom.
