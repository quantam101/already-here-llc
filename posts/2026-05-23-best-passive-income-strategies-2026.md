## What is Passive Income?
Before we dive into the strategies, let's define what passive income is. Passive income refers to earnings generated from investments, assets, or business ventures that require minimal effort to maintain. This can include anything from dividend-paying stocks to rental properties, and even online businesses. The key characteristic of passive income is that it can generate revenue without direct involvement, freeing up your time to focus on other pursuits.

### Benefits of Passive Income
The benefits of passive income are numerous. For one, it provides financial security and stability, allowing you to weather economic downturns and unexpected expenses. Additionally, passive income can provide the freedom to pursue your passions, travel, or simply enjoy a better work-life balance. With the right strategies in place, you can create a steady stream of passive income that lasts for years to come.
