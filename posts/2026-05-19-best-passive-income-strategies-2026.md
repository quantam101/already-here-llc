## What is Passive Income?
### Definition and Benefits
Passive income refers to the earnings generated from investments, businesses, or assets that require minimal effort to maintain. The benefits of passive income are numerous, including reduced financial stress, increased freedom, and a steady stream of revenue. With the trend velocity of passive income on the rise, it's essential to stay ahead of the curve and capitalize on the opportunities available.

### 4. Invest in Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs)
Investing in REITs is a passive income strategy that allows you to invest in real estate without directly managing properties.
