## What is Passive Income?
Passive income refers to the earnings generated from investments, assets, or business ventures that require minimal effort to maintain. Unlike traditional employment, where you trade time for money, passive income allows you to create a steady stream of revenue without actively working for it. This can include renting out properties, investing in dividend-paying stocks, or selling digital products.

### Types of Passive Income
There are several types of passive income, including:

* **Investment income**: earned through investments in stocks, bonds, or real estate investment trusts (REITs)
* **Rental income**: generated by renting out properties, such as apartments or vacation homes
* **Royalty income**: earned through the sale of intellectual property, such as books, music, or art
* **Dividend income**: received from owning shares in dividend-paying companies
* **Digital income**: generated through the sale of digital products, such as ebooks, courses, or software

## Top 10 Passive Income Strategies for 2026
Here are the top 10 passive income strategies that can help you achieve financial freedom in 2026:

1. **Invest in Dividend-Paying Stocks**: Invest in established companies with a history of paying consistent dividends. This can provide a relatively stable source of passive income, with some stocks offering yields of 4-6% or more.
2. **Create and Sell Digital Products**: Develop a digital product, such as an ebook, course, or software, and sell it through online platforms like Amazon or Udemy. With the right marketing strategy, you can earn significant passive income from digital product sales.
3. **Rent Out a Spare Room on Airbnb**: If you have an extra room in your home, consider renting it out on Airbnb. This can generate a steady stream of passive income, especially during peak travel seasons.
4. **Invest in Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs)**: REITs allow individuals to invest in real estate without directly managing properties. This can provide a steady source of passive income, with some REITs offering yields of 4-8% or more.
5. **Create a YouTube Channel or Twitch Stream**: Build a large following on YouTube or Twitch, and monetize your channel or stream with ads, sponsorships, or affiliate marketing. With the right content and audience, you can earn significant passive income from your online presence.
6. **Invest in Peer-to-Peer Lending**: Platforms like Lending Club and Prosper allow you to lend money to individuals or small businesses, earning interest on your investment. This can provide a relatively stable source of passive income, with some platforms offering returns of 6-8% or more.
7. **Create an Online Course or Coaching Service**: Share your expertise and create an online course or coaching service, selling it through platforms like Udemy or Skillshare. With the right marketing strategy, you can earn significant passive income from course sales or coaching services.
8. **Invest in a High-Yield Savings Account**: Some savings accounts offer high yields, often with minimal risk. This can provide a relatively stable source of passive income, with some accounts offering yields of 2-4% or more.
9. **Create a Mobile App or Game**: Develop a popular mobile app or game, and sell it through app stores like Apple or Google. With the right marketing strategy, you can earn significant passive income from app sales or in-app purchases.
10. **Invest in a Real Estate Crowdfunding Platform**: Platforms like Fundrise or Rich Uncles allow you to invest in real estate development projects or existing properties, earning passive income through rental income or property appreciation.
