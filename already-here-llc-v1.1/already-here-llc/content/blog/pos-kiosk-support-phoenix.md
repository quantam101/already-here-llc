Payment device and POS hardware work is high-volume, deadline-driven, and intolerant of ambiguity at closeout. A vendor dispatching field technicians across dozens of Arizona locations needs the field execution layer to handle the site work without requiring hand-holding — and to produce documentation that actually closes the ticket.

## What POS field support looks like when it works
The technician arrives within the scheduled window, follows the provided scope, handles the swap or install, verifies the device is operational, and delivers a closeout summary with photos where permitted. The dispatching team doesn't have to chase status or request documentation after the fact.

## Common POS and kiosk work types in the Phoenix market
Payment device replacement, POS terminal swaps, printer replacement and configuration, kiosk hardware troubleshooting, thin-client refresh work, and post-rollout remediation after missed or failed installations are the most common request types. Already Here LLC has documented execution history across QSR, retail, and hospitality environments in the Phoenix metro.
