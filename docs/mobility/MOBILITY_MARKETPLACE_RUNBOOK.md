# Already Here Mobility Marketplace Runbook

## Purpose

Create a two-sided Phoenix-centered marketplace connecting:

- owners with unused cars, vans, trucks, trailers, scooters, or other suitable vehicles;
- individuals needing short-term transportation or a delivery vehicle;
- businesses needing extra vehicles, drivers, routes, courier capacity, or delivery overflow;
- fleet operators, rental companies, dealers, repair shops, courier firms, restaurants, pharmacies, retailers, property managers, and logistics vendors.

The system captures and matches interest. It does not create a rental, lease, sale, employment, brokerage, delivery, or revenue-sharing agreement until the parties complete review and sign written terms.

## Required Screening Before Matching

### Vehicle supply

- registered owner or authorized business representative;
- financing or lease permits the proposed use;
- current registration;
- current insurance;
- condition and maintenance review;
- vehicle photos and mileage;
- known damage and warning lights;
- intended use and geographic limits;
- pricing or revenue-share expectation;
- pickup, return, cleaning, charging, fueling, mileage, and maintenance rules.

### Driver or renter

- identity and age verification through a secure later-stage process;
- valid license when required;
- driving-record and background requirements when applicable;
- platform eligibility for delivery work;
- insurance requirements;
- deposit/payment approval;
- safety orientation;
- before-and-after inspection acceptance.

### Business demand

- legal business name and contact;
- route and delivery requirements;
- cargo type, size, weight, and handling;
- service area and schedule;
- driver requirement;
- insurance and vendor onboarding requirements;
- expected volume and budget;
- payment terms;
- prohibited or regulated cargo.

## Match Scoring

Score each supply/demand pair from 0 to 100:

- vehicle-type fit: 20
- geography: 15
- schedule availability: 15
- cargo/use fit: 15
- budget/economics: 15
- insurance/compliance readiness: 10
- condition/reliability: 5
- relationship and repeat potential: 5

Only records scoring 70 or above should enter the manual agreement review queue.

## Outreach Sequence

### Vehicle-owner message

Subject: Put an unused vehicle to work or receive a purchase offer

Already Here LLC is building a Phoenix mobility network for owners with cars, vans, trucks, trailers, or scooters that are not being used. Depending on condition and fit, options may include a direct purchase discussion, rental or lease placement, managed fleet use, delivery use, or a written revenue-share arrangement. No commitment is created by submitting information. Ownership, insurance, condition, pricing, and written terms must be reviewed first.

### Business-demand message

Subject: Extra vehicle or delivery capacity for your operation

Already Here LLC is building a Phoenix mobility and delivery network for businesses that need temporary vehicles, drivers with vehicles, courier coverage, route support, or overflow delivery capacity. We are gathering route, cargo, schedule, service-area, insurance, and pricing requirements to determine fit. No service commitment begins until scope and written terms are approved.

### Courier/fleet partner message

Subject: Phoenix vehicle and delivery-capacity collaboration

Already Here LLC is organizing vehicle owners, drivers, scooter renters, and business delivery demand into a screened operating database. We are interested in reciprocal referrals, overflow routes, unused-vehicle placement, driver referrals, maintenance support, and fleet-capacity partnerships. We would like to compare service areas, vehicle needs, onboarding requirements, and partnership terms.

## Risk Controls

- Do not promise income or delivery-platform acceptance.
- Do not describe renters or delivery workers as employees unless formally hired and classified.
- Do not rent or place a vehicle without written insurance confirmation for the intended use.
- Do not use personal auto insurance assumptions for commercial delivery or rental use.
- Do not accept title, license, SSN, bank, policy-number, or other high-risk data through public intake.
- Do not broker regulated freight or represent carrier authority that Already Here does not hold.
- Do not handle controlled substances, hazardous materials, firearms, cash, or regulated medical specimens without specific authority, training, insurance, and written scope.
- Use an established peer-to-peer platform or obtain Arizona legal, tax, licensing, and insurance review before operating direct short-term vehicle rentals.
