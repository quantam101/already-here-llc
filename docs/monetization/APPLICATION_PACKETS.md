## Program Decision Questions

1. Is participation free?
2. Are direct client relationships allowed?
3. Can implementation fees be charged separately?
4. Is compensation recurring or one-time?
5. What is the attribution window?
6. What is the payout threshold and method?
7. Are email, QR code, social, and client referrals allowed?
8. Are trademark bidding or brand-keyword ads prohibited?
9. Can the company be both affiliate and agency/consulting partner?
10. What data-processing or privacy obligations apply?
