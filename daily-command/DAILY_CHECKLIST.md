# Daily Command Daily Checklist

Use this checklist to run the Daily Command Summary as a disciplined operating process.

## Pre-check

- Confirm current date.
- Confirm no private data will be committed to a public repository.
- Confirm approval gates are active.
- Confirm daily revenue target context: minimum $500 daily field revenue when scanning work.

## Run sequence

1. Scan AH/Hot.
2. Scan AH/Needs-Action.
3. Scan Field Nation.
4. Scan WorkMarket.
5. Process dispatch intake.
6. Scan procurement notices.
7. Scan billing/payment items.
8. Scan website/GitHub health.
9. Scan Drone/UAS opportunities.
10. Scan V2D Portal / Vets to Drones.
11. Scan free veteran drone training.
12. Scan FAA Part 107 pathways.
13. Scan paid drone work leads.
14. Scan AI lead-capture proof-of-work.
15. Scan vendor outreach and partner pipeline.

## Filtering checklist

For every candidate item, ask:

- Is this new or materially changed?
- Is there a deadline?
- Is money involved?
- Is revenue possible?
- Is payment at risk?
- Is production/security at risk?
- Is Stephen expected to approve or respond?
- Is there a clear exact next action?

Suppress the item when the answer is no and there is no strategic reason to keep it.

## Output checklist

The Daily Command Summary must include:

- Top 3 ranked actions.
- Only actionable or materially changed items.
- Source.
- Date.
- Deadline.
- Eligibility.
- Cost/free status.
- Apply/contact link if available.
- Grade.
- Risk flags.
- Exact next action.
- Approval gate when applicable.

## Closeout checklist

Before finalizing:

- Remove duplicates.
- Remove stale/low-value noise.
- Mark suspicious items clearly.
- Do not include raw private content in public-safe outputs.
- Make the top 3 actions explicit.
- Ensure every item has an exact next action.

## End-state

A completed Daily Command Summary should let Stephen decide what to do now, what to approve, what to pass, and what to monitor without rereading the entire inbox or GitHub notification stream.
