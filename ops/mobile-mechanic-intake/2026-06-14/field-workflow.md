# Mobile Mechanic Field Workflow

## Purpose

Protect Already Here LLC, the technician, the customer, and the partner/referral source by documenting vehicle condition before any on-site work starts.

## Before Starting Work

1. Confirm customer or site contact.
2. Confirm service location and safe work area.
3. Confirm requested service and approved scope.
4. Record year, make, model, plate, VIN, odometer, battery voltage/level, and fuel level.
5. Complete condition-upon-arrival checklist.
6. Capture required pre-work photos.
7. Note any visible damage, warning lights, leaks, missing parts, hazards, or customer concerns.
8. Obtain customer/site authorization when available.
9. Do not expand repair scope without written approval.

## Required Photo Set

Minimum required photos before work begins:

- Vehicle at service location.
- Front exterior.
- Rear exterior.
- Driver side exterior.
- Passenger side exterior.
- Interior/cabin.
- Dashboard, odometer, and warning lights.
- VIN tag or plate.
- Under hood / engine bay.
- Battery area.
- Close-ups of existing damage.
- Close-ups of the complaint area.

## Mechanic-Scope Boundary

Initial overflow outreach should avoid over-claiming repair capacity. Use the mechanic-adjacent support lane first unless an approved mechanic scope is clear.

Preferred first-lane work:

- Field documentation.
- VIN/odometer capture.
- Parts pickup and delivery.
- Site photos.
- Fleet unit intake.
- Customer or driver coordination.
- Closeout notes.
- Light roadside support only when approved in writing.

## Payment and Approval Controls

- Diagnostic minimum: $95.
- Local service-call minimum: $150.
- Mechanic labor floor: $75/hr.
- Customer-paid parts upfront.
- Written approval required before work begins.
- Written approval required before expanding scope.
- No warranty beyond stated approved work unless separately approved.

## Closeout

Before leaving site:

- Capture after-work photos where applicable.
- Record work performed.
- Record parts used.
- Record start/end time.
- Record unresolved issues.
- Send closeout notes and photos to dispatch/customer/partner as required.
