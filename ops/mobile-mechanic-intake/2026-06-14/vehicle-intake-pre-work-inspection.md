# Already Here LLC - Mobile Service Vehicle Intake & Pre-Work Inspection

Use before starting work to document vehicle condition, location, authorization, and required photos.

Contact: 602-882-2920 | dispatch@alreadyherellc@gmail.com

## 1) Job Information

- Date
- Arrival time
- Technician name
- Work order number
- Service location / address
- Customer name
- Phone
- Email
- Referred by / company

## 2) Vehicle Information

- Year
- Make
- Model
- Trim
- Color
- License plate / state
- VIN number
- Odometer
- Battery level / voltage
- Fuel level
- Keys provided: yes / no / N/A

## 3) Requested Service / Complaint

Capture the customer concern or requested work in plain language before touching the vehicle.

Required notes:

- What the customer says is wrong.
- What the customer wants performed.
- Whether the scope is diagnostic only, service only, or approved repair.
- Any limitations, symptoms, noises, warning lights, leaks, starting issues, or recent repairs.

## 4) Condition Upon Arrival Checklist

- Vehicle in park / safe position.
- Parking brake set.
- Engine off before initial walkaround unless diagnosis requires otherwise.
- No visible fluid leak under vehicle.
- No obvious smoke or burning smell.
- Battery terminals visually secure.
- Tires appear inflated and safe.
- Windshield damage noted.
- Body damage noted.
- Dashboard warning lights documented.
- Personal belongings visible inside vehicle noted.
- Pets or hazards at site disclosed.
- Customer present during walkaround when available.

## 5) Required Pre-Work Photos

These photos should be captured before work begins and attached to the work order or service record.

- Service location / vehicle at site.
- Front exterior.
- Rear exterior.
- Driver side exterior.
- Passenger side exterior.
- Under the hood / engine bay.
- Battery area.
- Interior front seats / cabin.
- Dashboard / odometer / warning lights.
- VIN tag / plate.
- Existing damage close-ups.
- Additional complaint-area photos.

## 6) Existing Damage / Notes

Document condition upon arrival, existing damage, missing parts, warning lights, leaks, or safety concerns.

Use this area to record:

- Dents, scratches, cracked glass, missing trim, broken panels, damaged lights, damaged tires, low fluids, disconnected components, visible leaks, smoke, smells, warning lights, and unsafe work conditions.
- Any area the customer specifically points out before work starts.
- Any reason a required photo could not be captured.

## 7) Authorization

Customer authorizes basic visual inspection, documentation photos, and approved on-site service. Any additional repair beyond the approved scope requires customer approval.

Signature lines:

- Customer signature / date.
- Technician signature / date.

## Operating Boundary

This form documents apparent vehicle condition and authorization before work begins. It is not a full mechanical diagnosis unless the service order separately states that a diagnostic service was approved.
